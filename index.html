Welcome to the Prahelika Quiz Forum

Established in 2021 during the height of the COVID-19 pandemic, the Prahelika Quiz Forum has rapidly emerged as a beacon of intellectual resilience, standing today as the only active quiz forum in the Darrang district and arguably Assam’s most prominent organization in the field. Although it is rooted in Darrang, the forum has expanded its mission to serve the entire state of Assam, dedicated to fostering a culture of curiosity and lifelong learning by organizing high-caliber events like its renowned annual Northeast India Quiz. By transforming information into a competitive sport during a time of global isolation, Prahelika successfully bridged the gap between academic education and general awareness, solidifying its reputation as a premier destination for the state's brightest minds to gather, compete, and grow.
